Hello, I'm Caliel Feijó ✶

Visual Artist from Recife, Pernambuco

About me

As a docent at the Museu Espaço Ciência – PE, I guide visitors through exhibits and provide educational experiences. I am also pursuing a degree in art education at the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE), and working as a freelance professional. I also hold a technical degree in Occupational Safety from the Federal Institute of Pernambuco (IFPE) and will start my undergraduate studies in Systems Analysis and Development at Embarque Digital in the near future.

My work is characterized by a multidisciplinary approach, encompassing the fields of art, education, and technology. I possess a steadfast commitment to education, research, and social impact, endeavoring to highlight the significance of Art within the domain of Information Technology and the professional sphere. Dedicated to the development of practices that democratize access to knowledge and reinforce culture as a vehicle for social transformation, I seek to redefine these learning environments. I firmly believe in the potential of this dialogue to expand perspectives, cultivate creativity, and stimulate discussions aimed at dismantling societal structures.

I am a product of the people, of music, of the arts, and of community engagement. My trajectory is deeply rooted in cultural expressions and a dedication to the appreciation of popular identities and knowledge. I uphold the conviction that education and art serve as potent forces for resistance and change. I engage in work, study, and creative endeavors with unwavering passion, consistently striving to effect positive change in my surroundings. I now feel prepared to extend this influence through the burgeoning field of technology.
